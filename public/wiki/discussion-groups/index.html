Summary

This page provides a list of email discussion groups and online forums about the UX disciplines and practice.

  • SIG-IA (Archives) - American Society for Information Science & Technology, IA Special Interest Group Discussion
  • IAI-Members (Archives) - Information Architecture Institute, Members' Discussion Group (Paid Membership Required)
  • IXDA (Archives) - Interaction Design Institute, Members' Discussion Group
  • UX Stackexchange - A general question and answer site for the UX disciplines.
