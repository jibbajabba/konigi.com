fontcapture.com: Create fonts from your handwriting

fontcapture might be interesting to people who create sketch-style wireframes, but don't like using Apple's Casual or Comic Sans. Simply download and fill in the font template, scan and upload it to fontcatpure, and download your completed font, usable on both Windows and Mac computers.
