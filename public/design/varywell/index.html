VaryWell


VaryWell is the portfolio of Seattle, Washington designers Ryan and Jason Hickner. The Hickners work on in web sites, interactive displays, and interesting hardware projects including a very cool LED light color mixer. Their portfolio is a nice demonstration of the the Flash Papervision tools. Selecting a project presents a stack of media that are unshuffled and spread around the screen, much as you would spread a set of slides on a lightbox. Click to focus on an image or video, and view that object’s details or watch that movie. Very nicely done.

http://varywell.com/
