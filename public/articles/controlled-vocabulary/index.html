Controlled Vocabulary

Definition

A collection of preferred terms that are used to assist in more precise retrieval of content. Controlled vocabulary terms can be used for populating attribute values during indexing, building labeling systems, and creating style guides and database schema. One type of a controlled vocabulary is a thesaurus.

Related topics: attribute values, indexing, labeling, preferred term, taxonomy, folksonomy, thesaurus
