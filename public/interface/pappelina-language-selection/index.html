Pappelina Language Selection

Pappelina, an interior design product company, provides a fun language selection page when you arrive at the site. Pick the hand with the ribbons in flag colors for Sweden for Swedish or UK/US for English. I know this is just a Flash splash page, but it's functional and a pretty entry page.
